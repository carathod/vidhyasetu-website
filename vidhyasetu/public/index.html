    About Us

    Vidhyasetu hostel - Gundiya

    Empowering underprivileged children with free shelter and education for a brighter future.

    Vidhyasetu Hostel, located in the small village of Gundiya in Dharmpur, Gujarat.It was established on 22/06/2021. It is a beacon of hope for underprivileged children who dream of a better future. Established with the mission to provide a safe and nurturing environment, our hostel offers a free home and education to children who cannot afford it. We believe that every child deserves the chance to learn, grow, and succeed, regardless of their background or financial situation.

    At Vidhyasetu Hostel, we are committed to empowering young minds by providing not just shelter, but also the educational support they need to thrive. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure that each child receives the care, attention, and opportunities they deserve, helping them to build a foundation for a brighter future. Through education and compassion, we aim to uplift our community, one child at a time.

    Vidhyasetu Hostel provides free shelter and education to underprivileged children in need, fostering a supportive environment for their growth and learning. Our mission is to bridge the gap to a brighter future through compassion and opportunity.

    Services

    Grocery

    At Vidhyasetu Hostel, we allocate approximately ₹30,000 each month towards providing essential groceries for our children. This budget ensures that our young residents receive nutritious and balanced meals, supporting their health and development. From fresh fruits and vegetables to grains, dairy products, and other staples, every rupee is spent with care to foster a well-rounded diet. Our commitment to quality and variety helps to create a homely atmosphere, where children feel cared for and valued, laying the foundation for their well-being and academic success.

    Transportation

    At Vidhyasetu Hostel, we invest approximately ₹6,000 each month in student transportation to ensure our children have safe and reliable access to their educational institutions. This budget covers the cost of commuting, including vehicle maintenance and fuel, enabling smooth and punctual travel for all students. By prioritizing dependable transportation, we help minimize disruptions to their learning experience and foster a consistent and supportive environment, allowing them to focus on their studies and personal growth without the stress of logistical challenges.

    Tuition and Stationery

    At Vidhyasetu Hostel, we allocate approximately ₹5,000 each month towards tuition and stationery for our students. This investment covers the cost of professional teachers who provide high-quality, personalized instruction, ensuring that each child receives the academic support they need to thrive. Additionally, it includes essential stationery supplies such as notebooks, pens, and educational materials, which are vital for their learning. By combining expert tuition with necessary resources, we create an enriching educational environment that supports our students' growth and success.
